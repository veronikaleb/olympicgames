Famous Olympic Athletes

United States


Simone Biles

Artistic gymnast. She won 11 Olympic and 30 World Championship medals. She the most decorated gymnast in history.

Michael Phelps

Competitive swimmer. He is the most successful and most decorated Olympian of all time with a total of 28 medals.

Katie Ledecky

Competitive swimmer. She has won nine Olympic gold medals and 21 world championship gold medals, the most in history for a female swimmer.


China


Ma Long

Professional table tennis player, six-time Olympic champion, and three-time World Champion.

Zou Shiming

Chinese former boxer and two-time Olympic champion.

Chen Ruolin

Chinese platform diver and coach. She is a five-time Olympic gold medalist, ten-time World championship medalist, nine-time World Cup medalist.


Ukraine


Olha Kharlan

Sabre fencer. She is a four-time individual women’s world sabre champion, six-time Olympic medalist and the most decorated Ukrainian Olympian in history.

Zhan Beleniuk

Greco-Roman wrestler. He is a three-time Olympic medalist, two-time world champion and a three-time European champion.

Liudmyla Luzan

Sprint canoeist. She is a two-time silver and a bronze Olympic medalist, three-time World champion and three-time European champion.


Brazil


Robert Scheidt

Brazilian sailor who has won two gold medals, two silver medals and a bronze from five Olympic Games and a Star Sailors League Final.

Rebeca Andrade

Artistic gymnast. Having won a total of six Olympic and nine World medals, she is the most decorated Brazilian and Latin American gymnast of all time.

Martine Grael

Brazilian sailor in the 49er FX class. Together with Kahena Kunze she won the 49er FX class at the 2014 ISAF Sailing World Championships and at the 2016 Rio Olympics, and 2020 Tokyo Olympics.


Japan


Kōhei Uchimura

Retired Japanese artistic gymnast. He is a seven-time Olympic medalist, winning three golds and four silvers, and a 21-time World medalist

Yui Ohashi

Retired Japanese swimmer, specializing in the medley events. She won Japan's second gold in the 2020 Summer Olympics, winning the Women's 400m individual medley. She also won the gold medal in the Women's 200m individual medley.

Naohisa Takato

Judoka. Takato is currently the top ranked judoka in the world in the extra-lightweight division. Takato won the bronze medal as Japan's extra-lightweight representative at the 2016 Olympics and won the gold medal in the same event at the 2020 Olympics held in Tokyo, Japan.


Australia


Emma McKeon

Retired Australian competitive swimmer. She is an eight-time world record holder, three current and five former, in relays. Her total career haul of 14 Olympic medals following the 2024 Olympic Games made her the most decorated Australian athlete.

Jessica Fox

Slalom canoeist. Her six olympic medals make her the most decorated canoe slalom paddler male or female, in Olympic history. Fox has won 22 medals at the ICF Canoe Slalom World Championships.

Ian Thorpe

Retired swimmer. He has won five Olympic gold medals, the second most won by any Australian after fellow swimmer Emma McKeon.
